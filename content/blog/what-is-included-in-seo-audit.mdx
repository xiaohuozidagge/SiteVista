## What Is an SEO Audit?

This demo article explains what goes into a professional SEO audit. Replace with your real content.

An SEO audit is a systematic review of your website's search engine visibility. It identifies issues that prevent your site from ranking well and provides a roadmap for improvement.

## Key Areas Covered

### Technical Analysis

The technical foundation of your site determines whether search engines can find, crawl, and index your content effectively.

### On-Page Review

On-page factors include everything visible to users — title tags, headings, content quality, and internal linking structure.

### Content Evaluation

Content is what ranks. A good audit evaluates whether your content matches search intent, uses appropriate keywords, and provides genuine value.

### Competitive Analysis

Understanding what your competitors are doing well — and where their gaps are — helps you prioritize your own SEO efforts.

## What to Expect From a Professional Audit

A manual audit goes beyond automated tool reports. You get context, prioritization, and actionable recommendations tailored to your specific situation.
