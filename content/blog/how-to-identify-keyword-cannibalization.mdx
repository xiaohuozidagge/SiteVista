## What Is Keyword Cannibalization?

This demo covers keyword cannibalization. Replace with your real content.

Keyword cannibalization happens when multiple pages on your site target the same search query. This confuses search engines and splits your ranking potential across multiple URLs.

## How to Find It

1. **Search Console**: Look for multiple URLs ranking for the same query
2. **Site search**: Use `site:yourdomain.com "keyword"` to find competing pages
3. **Content audit**: Review your title tags and H1s for overlap

## How to Fix It

- Merge competing pages into one comprehensive resource
- Differentiate targeting with more specific long-tail keywords
- Use canonical tags to consolidate ranking signals
- Redirect weaker pages to the strongest one
