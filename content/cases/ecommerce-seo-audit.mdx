## Overview [Demo]

This is a demo e-commerce SEO audit case study. Replace with your real audit content.

This example examines an online retailer selling home goods. The site has approximately 500 product pages across 20 categories.

## Key Findings

### Category Structure

The category structure was mostly logical, but several subcategories were too deep — products were 4-5 clicks from the homepage. Flattening the structure improved crawl efficiency.

### Product Page Optimization

Many product pages had thin content with manufacturer descriptions duplicated across the web. Unique product descriptions and user-generated content like reviews improved rankings.

### Technical Issues

Faceted navigation was generating thousands of duplicate URLs without proper canonical tags. Implementing correct canonicals and using `noindex` for filtered pages resolved the issue.

## Recommendations

1. **Flatten category structure** — Keep important products within 3 clicks of the homepage
2. **Add unique product descriptions** — Write original content for top-selling products
3. **Fix faceted navigation canonicals** — Prevent duplicate content from filtered URLs
4. **Add product structured data** — Implement Product schema with price, availability, and reviews
